Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot and cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a separate pan, heat olive oil over medium heat and saut√© the minced garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce and oregano to the pan, and simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. Once the pasta is cooked, drain it and toss it in the sauce mixture.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste, then serve with fresh basil leaves.
