Exploring Bali, Indonesia

Bali is a stunning island in Indonesia, known for its beautiful beaches, rich culture, and lush landscapes. Itâ€™s a place where you can relax on the beach, explore temples, and hike through scenic rice terraces.

Travel Tips for Bali

  • Always carry cash as many places don't accept cards.
  • Respect local customs and traditions, especially when visiting temples.
  • Make sure to wear sunscreen as the sun can be very intense.
  • Try the local food, especially Nasi Goreng and Satay.
